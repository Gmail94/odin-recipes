Grandma's Spaghtetti

A delicious homemade meal that always gives me a sense of home

The preferred meal when you need to make a delicious and quick meal".

Only the finest cuts of beef should be used, and is delicious when served immediately

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage. Regular or spicy
  • Onion and garlic
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Falvourings (basil, parsley, salt and pepper, red pepper flakes)
  • Spaghetti Noodles of your preference

Recipe instructions

  1. Cook Italian sausage. In a large pot, begin browning Italian sausage over medium heat, making sure to break up the meat into small crumbles. Add the chopped onion and cook until it’s softened.
  2. Add other ingredients and simmer. Stir in the garlic, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, water, basil, parsley, brown sugar, salt, crushed red pepper, and black pepper. Bring the meat sauce to a low boil. Stir in red wine. Simmer on low, stirring occasionally, for at least an hour.
  3. Cook the pasta. When you’re ready to eat and the meat sauce is about done cooking, cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  4. Serve and enjoy. Plate drained spaghetti noodles (or other favorite pasta) and spoon meat sauce over the top and sprinkle with parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley or fresh basil. Whip up a fresh Italian salad and garlic bread for a complete dinner!
