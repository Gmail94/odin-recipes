Grandma's Sheperds Pie

A delicious homemade meal that always gives me a sense of home

An Irish delicacy which reminds of us of our roots and turns our noses".

Always the finest when eaten with grandmother and is delicious when served with a sprinkle of fried onions on top.

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Corn
  • Beef broth
  • Worchester sauce

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Warm up the corn
  3. Add the ground beef, then the Worcestershire sauce and broth
  4. Mash cooked potatoes
  5. Layer the meat mixture and mashed potatoes in a casserole dish
  6. Bake in oven at 400Â°F until lightly brown
  7. Add fried onions on top of mashed potatoes
