An athentic version of Chicken Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Peanut or rice bran oil for frying
  • 4 eggs
  • Diced Sahllots
  • Fresh scallions or garlic chives chopped
  • Bean sprouts
  • Minced garlic
  • chicken
  • Dried red chili flakes
  • peanuts

Recipe instructions

  1. Heat large wok n high with oil
  2. Add shallots, chicken
  3. Remove meat when cooked and put on the side
  4. Add garlic and noodles with sauce until they become softened
  5. Add bean sprouts, pinch of garlic chives, and mix thoroughly
  6. Push noodles to one side, add egg and scramble
  7. Fold noodles over egg
  8. Plate noodles, add chicken, chili flakes and crushed peanuts
